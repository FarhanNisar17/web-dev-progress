Kashmir

"Kashmir, often referred to as 'Paradise on Earth,' is a land of mesmerizing beauty. With its snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush valleys, it is a haven for nature lovers. The vibrant culture, warm hospitality, and breathtaking landscapes make Kashmir a dream destination for travelers"

Food

"Harissa, a traditional Kashmiri delicacy, is a dish that embodies the warmth and richness of the region's cuisine. Made from slow-cooked mutton, wheat, and aromatic spices, this hearty dish is perfect for the chilly winters of Kashmir. Harissa is not just food; it is a symbol of Kashmiri heritage, often enjoyed with freshly baked bread and shared with loved ones."
